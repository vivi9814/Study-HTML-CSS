Recipe #1

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves: 1

    • -I don't know 'Serves' mean before.
    It means 'How many people is this dish for?'!

Ingredients Quantity
kimchi 5 spoons
rice 10 spoons
sugar 2 spoons
egg 1

Step 1: Fry kimchi in a frying pan.

Step 2: Shake kimchi with sugar .

Step 3: Put the rice in the result of step 2.

Step 4: Put the eggs in three stages and fry them together.

Complete! Eat yammy!
